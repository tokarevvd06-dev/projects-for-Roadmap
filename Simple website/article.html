Why did I choose the direction of "Frontend developer"?

I chose Frontend development because I am interested in seeing the results of my work immediately in the form of interfaces and thinking about how the pages will work for real users. For me, this is more interesting than working with databases or other backend development tasks.

I like that at first glance frontend may seem like a simple task — there are designers and ready-made solutions that quickly create a website. But in order to make an industrial, reliable product, working with the interface requires a lot: architecture, logic, scalability, high-quality error handling. Therefore, it is interesting to dive into this area.
